Welcome to MobiHoc 2018

MobiHoc 2018, the Nineteenth International Symposium on Mobile Ad Hoc Networking and Computing will be held in Los Angeles, USA in June 2018. MobiHoc is a premier international symposium dedicated to addressing challenges in dynamic networks and computing.

It will bring together researchers and practitioners from a broad spectrum of networking research to present the most up-to-date results and achievements in the field.

MobiHoc 2018 will feature a highly selective technical program, multiple distinguished keynote addresses, and an exciting panel. In addition, it includes workshops that are focused on areas of emerging interest.
